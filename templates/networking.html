The Money, Politics and Transparency Project is intended to provide an opportunity for advocates, researchers, party officials, regulators, journalists and others to collaborate and network on issues related to political finance transparency. To receive updates and join in discussions please:

  • Join the Money, Politics and Transparency Google Group.
  • Follow @MPTransparency
  • Contact us about contribution to the creation of political finance transparency norms.

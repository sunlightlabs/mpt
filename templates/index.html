Money, Politics and Transparency
is dedicated to improving political finance transparency around the globe.

The Money, Politics and Transparency Project will provide a forum to share best (and worst) practices, reform efforts, in-depth research, analysis, and case studies to a network of global actors, ultimately co-creating a set of transparency norms that can be used by stakeholders to improve political finance systems in their own countries, fostering accountability and combating corruption.
